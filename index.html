How SYNAPSE works

  • Add your friends and pick a category of real-world problems — changing oil, surviving without a phone, spotting a scam.
  • Shout It: a question appears, the mic listens, first to yell the right answer wins the point.
  • Explain It: everyone gets a timer to explain their answer out loud. The judge ranks who was most accurate.
  • Get it wrong? Expect a roast — then the real answer, so you actually learn it.
  • The whole point: make decisions without reaching for AI. Keep the brain firing.
