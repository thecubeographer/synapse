How it works

  • Add your friends, choose a mode, pick a category of real-world questions.
  • Shout It: a question appears, the mic listens, first to yell the right answer earns a tally.
  • Explain It: everyone gets a timer to explain out loud; the judge ranks who was most accurate.
  • Get it wrong? Expect a roast — then the real answer, so you actually learn it.
  • Phone flips to landscape during play. The point: decide things without AI.
