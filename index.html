How it works

  • Add your friends and pick a category of real-world problems.
  • Shout It: a question appears, the mic listens, first to yell the right answer wins the point.
  • Explain It: everyone gets a timer to explain out loud; the judge ranks who was most accurate.
  • Get it wrong? Expect a roast — then the real answer, so you actually learn it.
  • The point: make decisions without reaching for AI.
